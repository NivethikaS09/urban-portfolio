Urban Informatics & Planning

Planning Better Places Through Data, Design, and Collaboration

As a First Class BSc (Hons) graduate in Urban Informatics and Planning from the University of Moratuwa, I specialize in urban planning, GIS, spatial analysis, and development coordination. My academic background and professional experience have enabled me to contribute to planning projects ranging from development assessments and site analysis to GIS mapping and research.

I believe successful planning integrates technical knowledge, community needs, and sustainable development principles to create places that improve quality of life. By linking geospatial intelligence with architectural design sensibilities, I translate complex spatial datasets into insights that shape urban forms.

"Good planning goes beyond designing spaces—it creates opportunities, strengthens communities, and shapes sustainable environments. I believe that integrating planning knowledge with spatial technologies enables better decisions and more resilient cities."
— Nivethika Sivagnanaseelan
